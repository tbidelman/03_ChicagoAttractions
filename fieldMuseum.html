The Field Museum


1400 S Lake Shore Dr,
Chicago, IL 60605-2496

Description

One of America's premier natural history museums, the Field's "Live over Time" exhibit now houses "Sue," the largest and most complete Tyrannosaurus rex ever discovered.

"This was one of my favourite places in Chicago. There are so many things to see that it's impossible to do in a day. Grab a map and see the halls you want to see the most, and do those first. Highly recommend the 'Evolving Planet' hall with the dinosaurs."

--rhonhagedorn
