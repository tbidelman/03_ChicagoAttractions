Millenium Park


201 E Randolph St, Between Michigan Ave. & Columbus Ave.,
Chicago, IL 60602

Description

Three square blocks of open lakefront complete with a Ferris Wheel, outdoor music pavillion and ice skating rink; expected to become one of the city's most popular sites.

"Go see this free park! Go during the day and go especially in the evening. Many of the pieces were lit and had changing patterns of light combined with water. It was a lot of fun to explore and see what was around the corner."
